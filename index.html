Our Story

Founded in 2019, Belmont Tattoo was built on a passion for creative expression and top-tier craftsmanship. With a diverse team of artists specializing in styles ranging from traditional and neo-traditional to realism and fine line, we've earned a reputation for quality and care. Every piece we create is a collaboration—between artist, client, and inspiration.
